What do we play?

We play more or less everything at Wolfe's Den. We have a heavy focus on Dungeon & Dragons, Warhammer, and Magic: The Gathering, but also have a wide group who enjoyes

  • Other RPG Systems like StarFinder
  • Board and other Card Games
  • Video Games
  • Miniature Painting
  • 3D Printing

We are also just a group of people who like to do fun things together. Suggest something in one of our groups and someone will likely want to join you.
